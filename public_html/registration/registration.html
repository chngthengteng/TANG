How to register

  • Register all your family members at the same time by clicking "Add Registrant" (not "Finish and Pay") at the end of each application.

  • If you need to live with your spouse/family or have rooming needs, we’ll ask you to complete an occupancy request at the end.

  • If you have questions while you’re registering, come back to this FAQ at any time.  Simply scroll up on the page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register late?

You can register late online from June 1st -  June 10th at a rate of $400 per person.  After June 10th, registration is officially closed. No exceptions.

What if I can't stay for the whole conference?

Email us at hello@tangeneration.org and tell us more about your needs.


Rooming

Who should request a room?

Spouses and families with children under 6 or elderly should complete the occupancy request.  TANG youth campers High School and below generally should not request a room. If you have special circumstances, ask us at hello@tangeneration.org.

Is my room guaranteed?

Priority will be given to elderly and families with young children. We have limited 3+ rooms available, so we may not be able to accomodate all large group requests.


Discounts

Family Discount

If you have a family of 4 or more, register them all in the same registration session and you’ll automatically receive a discount of $10 per user.
